About Me

Learning through people, designing through research.

My Approach

Hi, I'm Cindy! I'm passionate about understanding people—what drives them, what challenges they face, and how we can create solutions that truly make a difference. My background in psychology and social science gives me a unique lens for UX research, where I focus on uncovering the "why" behind user behaviors and translating those insights into actionable design decisions.

My Focus

I believe the best research doesn't just solve immediate problems—it creates long-term impact. Whether I'm designing surveys for institutional research, evaluating nonprofit programs, or conducting user interviews, I'm always thinking about how to bridge user needs with organizational goals. I thrive in collaborative environments where diverse perspectives come together to create meaningful change.
